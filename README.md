# Nykaa Marketing Analytics Dashboard

### Project Overview

This project focuses on marketing performance analysis for Nykaa using Excel-based analytics and dashboard reporting. The objective was to transform raw campaign data into meaningful business insights through data cleaning, pivot analysis, Power Pivot measures, and interactive dashboard creation.

The dashboard helps evaluate campaign effectiveness, channel performance, revenue contribution, conversion trends, customer segmentation, and ROI across multiple marketing channels.

---

### Project Objective

* Analyze marketing campaign performance across multiple channels.
* Measure revenue, profit, leads, and conversion metrics.
* Identify top-performing channels and campaign types.
* Track monthly revenue trends.
* Build an interactive Excel dashboard using slicers and pivot-based analysis.

---

### Key Insights

* Google generated the highest ROI among marketing channels.
* Social Media campaigns produced the highest conversions.
* Revenue trend showed strong monthly consistency except seasonal dips.
* YouTube and Instagram contributed significantly to conversion rates.
* Leads and conversions reduced through funnel stages, highlighting customer drop-off.

---

### Analysis & Findings

* Revenue performance varied by campaign type.
* Paid campaigns delivered higher conversion contribution.
* Certain customer segments contributed more to revenue.
* Marketing funnel analysis identified areas for campaign optimization.
* ROI comparison highlighted cost-efficient channels.

---

### Recommendations

* Increase investment in high-performing channels like Google and Social Media.
* Optimize low-performing campaigns.
* Improve lead nurturing to reduce funnel drop-off.
* Focus on high-conversion customer segments.
* Use monthly trend analysis for campaign planning.
